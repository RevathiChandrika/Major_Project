ABOUT DECOVISION

DECOVISION is an intelligent home styling assistant designed to redefine how individuals approach interior design. Leveraging the power of artificial intelligence, our platform provides users with tailored décor recommendations, interactive style quizzes, and advanced visual planning tools — all aimed at creating a personalized and inspiring home environment.
    Whether you're furnishing a new apartment, redesigning a single room, or simply exploring ideas for future upgrades, DECOVISION offers a seamless experience. Our system analyzes your preferences, such as color schemes, material choices, lighting ambiance, and spatial layout, to deliver curated furniture and décor suggestions that align with your vision and budget. With integrated AI chat support and access to a wide range of products from both global and local retailers, DECOVISION ensures that your interior design journey is not only efficient but also enjoyable. Users can explore recommended styles, visualize changes through AI-generated room previews, and make confident design decisions — all in one unified platform.
    At its core, DECOVISION empowers users to bring their dream spaces to life through intelligent guidance, creativity, and convenience. Whether your style leans toward modern minimalism, cozy boho, or classic luxury, DECOVISION transforms inspiration into reality — beautifully, intelligently, and effortlessly.
